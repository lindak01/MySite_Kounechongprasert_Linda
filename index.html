My Favs

If you're anything like me, every time you walk into a MAC store you feel like a kid in a candy shop with only $1 to spend. Beauty comes at a price and although I would love to be able to have and review all the holiday kits, I simply cannot afford to. However, I did get my hands on a few kits and I will review my favorites among them.

First kit I will review is the Fabulousness Neutral Eyes Kit ($39.50 for 0.14 oz.). This kit is beautiful. It comes in a cute little satin-tuft compact with five neutral eyeshadows.

  • Brun Muted blackish-brown (Satin)
  • Blonde Mink Grey taupe frost (Lustre)
  • Enviable Cool mint green with pearl (Veluxe Pearl)
  • Smut Muted black with red shimmer (Velvet)
  • Magical Mist Metallic taupe (Luster)

These colors are perfectly coordinated and great for everyday work wear. The colors in this palette are very subtle. Blonde Mink has a bit of fall out when applied but it is not that bad. Magical Mist offers a bit of sparkle. Brun is a dark black brown perfect for contouring. Enviable is my personal favorite color from this palette and is very pretty on the lid. Smut is a great color with a bit of red shimmer. All in all this palette gets my recommendation. However I do have to mention that the amount of product that you get in this kit gets a thumbs down.
